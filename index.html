EXPERT SOLUTIONS FOR COMPLEX PROBLEMS

Who are we?

Forensix Design is a professional service firm owned by founding members Chris Kahanek, PE, SE, AIA and Forensix Consulting, LLC. The team at Forensix Consulting provides significant expertise and manpower support for Forensix Design.

What do we do?

We are a professional service firm practicing civil engineering, structural engineering, and architecture. We focus on design services for existing properties with deterioration, damage, and deficiencies. Services offered in collaboration with Forensix Consulting include:

  • Storm, fire, and earthquake damage repair design
  • Seismic retrofit design
  • Historic building evaluation and restoration design
  • California balcony (EEE) inspections
  • Seismic evaluations (ASCE 41 Tier 1/2/3)
  • Nonstructural seismic evaluations (FEMA E-74)
  • Property Condition Assessments (ASTM E2018)
  • Evaluations for HOA Reserve Studies
  • Property claim response
  • Builder's Risk claim response
  • Construction & design defect litigation support
  • Code compliance consulting
  • Vibration analysis
  • Dispute resolution
  • Trial testimony
  • Multi-discipline project management

Why are we better?

Communication is the single most important factor driving the success of construction projects. We believe in frequent, thorough communication about issues as they arise. We utilize advanced field- and web-based technologies on all projects to enhance communication.

From a technical perspective, we embrace challenges that are ignored or overlooked by traditional boundary-focused design professionals. 'Nonstructural' structure, unusual flashing and waterproofing, ground-supported concrete slabs, etc.

What is our experience?

Forensix Design is new and small, but it is built upon decades of experience at multi-practice international engineering firms. Examples of projects in our portfolio:

  • Iconic museeums, performance, and entertainment venues
  • Elementary schools in California (DSA)
  • Grading and drainage for large mixed-use developments
  • Multi-story and unreinforced masonry buildings with severe earthquake damage
  • An industrial facility severely damaged by explosion and fire
  • Post-tensioned towers and parking structures

Where are we?

Forensix Design will find a way to help wherever you need us. We have extensive past work experience outside of the United States, from Dubai to New Zealand to Mexico, and we pursue international opportunities with enthusiasm.

Forensix has offices in North and Central Texas, Phoenix, and Southern California.

Why 'SIX'?

The name of ForenSIX was inspired by the 'Six Pillars of Character', the core ethical values identified by an organization called Character Counts!. We believe these values are an excellent moral base for professional service firms like Forensix.

Trustworthiness

We strive to be honest in all situations and honor our commitments. This can a challenge in an industry full of suprises. We address the unexpected with thorough, clear communication.

Respect

Our work can involve contentious and adversarial situations. We maintain a tone of respect in all interactions and believe in the healing power of courtesy and empathy.

Responsibility

We hold ourselves accountable and expect the same from others. Professional engineers are guardians of the health, safety, and welfare of the public, and this duty is held above profit or personal gain.

Fairness

We typically have more technical knowledge than our clients, and we are careful to not take advantage. We obey the laws of the land and the ethical rules of our profession.

Caring

Construction is a business full of sharp elbows. We rise above the fray by being kind and compassionate and expressing gratitude when others treat us well in return.

Citizenship

We have specialized knowledge that can benefit our local community. We contribute to our local school, community, and religious organizations.
